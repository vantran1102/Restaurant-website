ABOUT US

Star Anise is family owned and operated. We believe that food is what brings people together. We want to cook for our patrons as we would for our family. You won't find msg in our kitchen and we use locally sourced produce when as much as possible. We are not the pho shop that you are used to. Nothing is mass produced. Small batches made in house. Everything from broths to specialty sauces and even our mayo and pâté is made by hand. Here at Star Anise, traditional flavors and techniques meets the modern palate. We are proud to be able to share our flavors with our patrons! Bon appetit.
